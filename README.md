# VOIDRUNNER

A juicy neon arcade survival shooter you play right in your browser. Weave through the swarm, fight **WARLORD** bosses, grab power-ups, and chase a high score.

**▶ Play: https://samuelorcutt1998-pixel.github.io/voidrunner/**

## How to play

- **Move** — mouse, touch-drag, or WASD / arrow keys
- **Fire** — automatic, so focus on positioning and dodging
- **Blast** the swarm, **dodge** what you can't kill (asteroids take 3 hits)
- Grab power-ups: ⚡ Rapid fire · ✳ Spread shot · ⬡ Shield
- Build a **kill streak** to raise your score multiplier — it resets when you're hit
- Every **500,000 points** a WARLORD boss warps in; beat it and grab the gold **+1 life** it drops
- You have **3 lives** (up to 5); the screen glows red on your last one

High score is saved locally in your browser. Toggle sound with the ♪ button.

## Tech

A single self-contained `index.html` — HTML5 Canvas, no dependencies, no build step, works offline.
